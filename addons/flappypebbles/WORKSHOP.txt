Torture Five Pebbles with your favorite 2013 mobile game! Add-on for Five Pebbles Pong.
If this is the only addon, you can grab the 4th pearl from the left to start this game.

You can program and upload your own games! This is an example.
Source code is here: https://github.com/woutkolkman/fivepebblespong/tree/master/addons
